From Statutes to Startups

I’m a law student with a big heart for tech and even bigger dreams for justice. What started as a passion for writing case briefs and learning courtroom etiquette slowly evolved into something deeper a desire to build systems that actually work for people. I spend my mornings grounding myself in faith and structure, my days learning to code and design smarter legal tools, and my nights dreaming up ways to make the law feel less scary and more human. This space is my attempt to merge it all,law, creativity, tech, and service , into something that can truly help people.

My Career So Far

My career journey is rooted in legal education and fueled by innovation. I've interned in both legal offices and research roles, sharpening my understanding of legal systems and client needs. Alongside this, I earned a certificate in web development and immersed myself in tools like Webflow, GPT, and Airtable to prototype legal tech platforms. My work blends legal precision with human-centered design, especially focused on helping women, youth, and underserved communities. Whether drafting legal memos or designing no-code solutions, I’m always working toward the same goal: expanding access to justice through creativity, clarity, and technology.
